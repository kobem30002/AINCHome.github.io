Seon Man Kim

received a PhD degree from the School of Information and Mechatronics from Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology (GIST), South Korea in 2013. During 2013–2015, He was a researcher at the Institute of Sound and Vibration Research (ISVR), University of Southampton, UK. From October 2015 to May 2017, he was a researcher at GIST. During June 2017–November 2018, he was a senior professional researcher at UI/UX research team at LG Display CTO. From December 2018 to August 2024, he was a senior research engineer at the Spatial Optical Information Research Center at the Korea Photonics Technology Institute. Since September 2024, he has been with the School of Computing and Artificial Intelligence at Hanshin University, South Korea, as an assistant professor. His current research interests include AI solutions in the field of audio, speech and hearing devices.
